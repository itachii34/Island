Five fun loving travelers Henry, Mitch, Rob, Fay and Mike document their travels from New York to Malaysia on the journey of a lifetime in search of Henry’s family roots from the Iban tribe in Borneo. Meeting some interesting characters along the way, they have the adventure of a lifetime, only to discover the tribe has crossed the boarder into the unfamiliar territory of Indonesian tribes. As the group heads deeper into the jungle they begin to realize unfamiliar territory is not somewhere you want to be on the island of Borneo.
